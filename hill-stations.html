Nearest Hill Stations from Ujala Hospital, Kashipur

  • Nainital: ~75 km away | 2.5 hours drive | Famous for its beautiful lake, boating, and bustling Mall Road.
  • Bhimtal: ~85 km away | 2.5 to 3 hours drive | A peaceful alternative to Nainital with a larger, calmer lake.
  • Ranikhet: ~115 km away | 4 hours drive | A serene cantonment town surrounded by pine forests and mountain views.
  • Almora: ~125 km away | 4.5 hours drive | A cultural hub known for traditional Kumaoni heritage and local sweets.
  • Kausani: ~165 km away | 5.5 hours drive | Offers spectacular, uninterrupted panoramic views of the Trishul and Nanda Devi peaks.
  • Mussoorie: ~210 km away | 5.5 hours drive | The "Queen of the Hills" featuring colonial charm and waterfalls.
